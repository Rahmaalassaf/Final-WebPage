Facial Recognition Instructions:

Step One:

Press "E" on the keyboard to access the face mesh

Step Two:

Start connecting the dots on the face mesh to draw the desired shape

Step Three:

Control and maneuver the color slider on the right to change the colors of your shape

// --- If you happen to make a mistake you can simply press "D" on the keyboard to delete the shape --- //
Step Four:

Press "C" on the keyboard to complete the shape you created

Step Five:

Press "E" on the keyboard again to remove the face mesh and access the drawing on your face

Step Six:

Press "S" on the keyboard to take a screenshot of your design!
